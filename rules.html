How It Works

Share your version of one of ALDO's Spring 13 Trends.

Upload your entry photo to the ALDO On Style Facebook App. Your photo can be:

  • A picture of your outfit
  • A picture of a friends outfit
  • A soda can
  • Anything that inspires you about one of our Spring Trends
  • *But remember, they have to be your own original images!
  • Tag it in one of the 3 ALDO Trends:
  • Plastic Fantastic
  • Culture Clash
  • Virtual Dreams
  • Share it with friends
  • Click the Love It button for the other submissions you enjoy!
  • Tune it regularly to see new submissions and to find out if you've won!
